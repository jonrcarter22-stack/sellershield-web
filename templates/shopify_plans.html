Frequently asked questions

What happens after the free trial?

At the end of your 7-day trial you'll be charged your plan's monthly rate via Shopify Billing. You can cancel before then with no charge.

Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What counts as a "channel"?

Google Shopping, Amazon Seller Central, and Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Shopping are the three supported channels. Each requires different compliance rules.

What does "auto-fix" mean?

Auto-fix automatically creates or updates missing policy pages on your store using your shop's info — no manual copy-paste required. One-click fixes apply product-level corrections with a single button press.
